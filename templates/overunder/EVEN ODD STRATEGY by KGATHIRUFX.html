EVEN MARKET STRATEGY

Prerequisites:

  • GREEN BAR must be on Even Market
  • Digit with green bar should be 12.1% or higher
  • Minimum three even numbers should have 10% or above
  • RED BAR should be on Even market

Manual Trading Entry Point

1. Wait for cursor to hit EVEN digit (>10%)

2. When cursor enters digit with GREEN BAR

3. As digit increases, click EVEN

Bot Trading Entry Point

1. Wait for cursor to hit two ODD numbers

2. Wait for hit on digit with green bar (EVEN)

3. RUN your BOT

ODD MARKET STRATEGY

Prerequisites:

  • Digit with red bar should be 12.1% or higher
  • Minimum three red numbers should have 10% or above
  • RED BAR should be on Odd market

Manual Trading Entry Point

1. Wait for cursor to hit ODD digit (>10%)

2. When cursor enters digit with GREEN BAR

3. As digit increases, click ODD

Bot Trading Entry Point

1. Wait for cursor to hit two EVEN numbers

2. Wait for hit on digit with green bar (EVEN)
